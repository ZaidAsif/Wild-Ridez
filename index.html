How Does This Thing Work?

In today's fast-paced world, you've got places to be but not enough time. Wouldn't it be nice if there were a transportation service that changed the way you get around? Introducing Wild Ridez — an innovative ride-sharing service that helps people reach their destination faster and hassle-free. Getting started is as easy as tapping a button in our app.

Download The App

Head over to the app store and download the Wild Ridez app. You're just a few taps away from getting your ride.

Request A Unicorn

We can get you there. Simply request a ride on the app and we'll connect you with a unicorn immediately.

Pick A Price

Pick the valuation you're willing to pay and your ride is set up. The only surge is the acceleration when you take off.

Ride Off To Success!

After matching with your unicorn and agreeing to terms, you'll be all set. Your unicorn will arrive shortly to pick you up.

Our Story

Wild Ridez was started by a former hedge fund analyst and a software developer. The two long-time friends happened upon the idea after attending a silent yoga retreat in Nevada. After gazing upon the majestic herds of unicorns prancing across a surreal Nevada sunset, they witnessed firsthand the poverty and unemployment endemic to that once proud race. Whether it was modern society's reliance on science over magic or not, we'll never know the cause of their Ozymandian downfall and fade to obscurity. Moved by empathy, romance, and free enterprise, they saw an opportunity to marry society's demand for faster, more flexible transportation to underutilized beasts of labor through an on-demand market making transportation app. Using the founders' respective expertise in animal husbandry and software engineering, Wild Ridez was formed and has since raised untold amounts of venture capital. Today, Wild Ridez has thousands of unicorns in its network fulfilling hundreds of rides each day.
